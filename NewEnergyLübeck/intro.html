Our mission is to arouse your awareness of the new energy

On behalf of environmental friendliness, this website aims to encourage users to consume electricity generated by more of the new energy power plants, including solar and wind power. We strictly modify the calculation rules so that you can clearly see whether the new energy power plants are currently doing a good job or not. Also, an hourly estimation of future 48 hours is available. All of this are integrated into a signal function where the plain data are visualized vividly.

The signal light function

The rule is very simple. While the data comes out a red light, probably the new energy power plants are not even working due to the low power. Shutting down some of the electronic devices is recommended which can evidently loosen the burden of the fossil plants. Yellow light comes out when the new energy power plants are working not very efficiently which means it is better to use only appliances with not too high electricity consumption. Finally, if the green light is on, that stands for new energy power plants dealing considerable electricity amount. Now is the best time to consume electricity while being environmental friendly.
